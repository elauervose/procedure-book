# Launch Procedures

## Basic Powerup

 1. Get rocket on pad
 1. Attach umbilical
 1. Run LTC client
 1. Turn on shore power
 1. Turn on FC
 1. Turn on WiFi
 1. Turn on ATV

## Systems Checkout

 1. Trackmaster on
 1. ssh into rocket over ATG link
 1. Run FCF
 1. Verify
    - data link
    - logfiles
    - video
    - video recording

## Terminal Count

 1. Verify all power is on
 1. Clear area for launch
 1. Start RSO/LCO integration
 1. Restart loggers
 1. Turn off shore power
 1. Check ground
    - Telemetry
    - Video
    - TeleMetrium
 1. Check GPS lock
 1. Get in positions
    - Recovery eyes up
    - Cameras set
    - Trackmaster and spotter
 1. Go/No-go poll
    - LTC
    - Recovery
    - TeleMetrium
    - Video
    - Trackmaster
    - Telemetry
    - FCF
    - FD
 1. **ARM** rocket
 1. Start countdown
 1. Send ignite signal
